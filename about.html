About

Simon(L) and Terry(R) with Sam Spade
of Midnight Runners

Simon and Terry, the rock ‘n’ roll dream team behind Searchlight Promotions, boast a combined 25 years of experience in the wild world of promoting rock and metal gigs. These guys aren’t just sitting around waiting for talent to come knocking; they’re out there like musical treasure hunters, passionately scouring the land for up-and-coming unsigned rock and metal bands for Norwich.

...but wait there's more...

Whether they're at other gigs or festivals across the country, Simon and Terry are on a mission to find the next big thing in music. They’re like the Indiana Jones of the rock scene, minus the fedora but definitely rocking some killer leather jackets. With their eyes peeled and ears wide open, they’re dedicated to shining a spotlight on those hidden gems ready to unleash their sound on the world! Simon and Terry, kicked off their own epic journeys in the music scene: Simon with his brainchild - POWer Promotions, and Terry launching the intergalactic vibes of Alien Nation.

But when they decided to join forces, that's when the magic happened!

Enter Searchlight Promotions, a powerhouse duo that shines a spotlight on the freshest talent in the biz. Their mission? To champion unsigned musicians and bands spanning everything from classic rock to ear-splitting heavy metal. These sonic shenanigans take place mainly at The Brickmaker's and B2 Venue in Norwich.

Riding high on their success with Searchlight Promotions, our dynamic duo birthed ROXOFF, a one-day music festival that kicked off in 2024. This.Is.A.Wild.Indoor.Bash at The Brickmaker's where the air is thick with excitement and the sound of guitars shredding fills your ears.

The dream is to transform ROXOFF into an epic outdoor, two-day festival complete with camping and all the glorious chaos that comes with it—new friendships forged over cold beers while headbanging to emerging rock and metal bands in the glorious British weather.
